Little Women. is a novel by American author Louisa May Alcott
which was originally published in two volumes. Alcott wrote
the book over several months at the request of her publisher.
The story follows the lives of the four March sisters Meg, Jo,
Beth, and Amy and details their passage from childhood to
womanhood. It is loosely based on the lives of the author and
her three sisters. Scholars classify it as an
autobiographical or semi autobiographical novel.
